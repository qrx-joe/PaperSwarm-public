# Revised Paper Placeholder for Public Demo

This public demo package includes the review trace, conflict report, author
resolution, revision plan, verification report, archive summary, and EvoMap
bundle for the cached medical RCT example.

The full revised manuscript is intentionally not included in this sanitized
public artifact. The demo focuses on the auditable review workflow rather than
redistributing a complete manuscript draft. The revision outcome is documented
in:

- `runs/demo/advice/revision_plan.md`
- `runs/demo/revise/revise_report.md`
- `runs/demo/archive/review_archive.md`

For a private/local run, this file is produced by the `revise` step after the
Worker/Verifier loop applies the accepted revision plan.
